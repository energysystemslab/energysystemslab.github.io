Damilola is a Computer Engineering student at Wichita State University. She joined the Energy Systems Lab as an undergraduate research student to work on a project titled "Cloud-connected battery management: leveraging Raspberry Pi for real-time state of health monitoring and analysis". Through her work in the Energy Systems Lab, she has published several journal articles as a co-author, including in ChemElectroChem and IEEE Transactions on Industry Applications.
