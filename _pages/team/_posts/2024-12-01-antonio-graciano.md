Antonio is an undergraduate student at Wichita State University. He joined the Energy Systems Lab as an undergraduate research student to work on a project titled "Characterization of electric field enhancement due to gaseous voids in high-Voltage insulators". Through his work as in the Energy Systems Lab, Antonio is the recipient of the 3rd place best research poster at the Annual IEEE Wichita Holiday Banquet (IEEE Wichita section, region 5).
